Privacy Policy

Luxe Beauty Lounge ("we," "us," or "our") operates this website and the salon at 1234 J Street, Sacramento, CA 95814. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or contact us.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number when you submit our booking or contact forms
  • Appointment details: service preferences, preferred dates, and any notes you include in your request
  • Usage data: standard website analytics including pages visited, time on site, and referral source (collected via analytics tools)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your appointment requests and inquiries
  • Confirm and manage your bookings
  • Send service reminders or follow-ups if you have opted in
  • Improve our website and services based on usage patterns

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Contact Form & Email

When you submit our contact or booking form, your message is delivered to us via EmailJS, a third-party email delivery service. EmailJS processes your submission securely. We do not store form submissions on our servers. Please review EmailJS's privacy policy for details on their data handling.

Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use the site. These tools collect anonymized data including page views, device type, and geographic region. You can opt out of analytics tracking through your browser settings or by using a browser extension such as the Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on.

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the information you submit. Our website uses HTTPS encryption. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information through our site, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

Your Rights

If you would like to review, update, or request deletion of personal information you have provided to us, please contact us at hello@luxebeautylounge.com or call (916) 555-0100. We will respond within a reasonable time frame.
